What is diamond fluorescence, and how does it affect a diamond's value?

Fluorescence occurs when a diamond exhibits a visible glow under UV light. While it doesn't drastically affect the diamond's value, some buyers prefer diamonds with fluorescence, while others do not. It's all about personal preference.

What is a diamond cut, and why is it important?

Diamond cut refers to the quality of the diamond's facets and how they are arranged. A well-cut diamond has better light reflection, making it sparkle more and appear more brilliant.
