Discover Diamond Shapes

Round

Tejani Diam showcases exquisite craftsmanship with our round diamonds, designed to deliver unmatched brilliance. The round brilliant cut is crafted using precision techniques to amplify its sparkle and timeless beauty.

Oval

The oval cut diamond by Tejani Diam is a true blend of sophistication and modern charm. Its elongated design provides a unique look while maximizing its brilliance and appearing larger than a round diamond of similar carat weight.

Pear

Our pear-shaped diamonds stand out with their unique silhouette, combining classic brilliance with a tapered edge. Perfect for creating elegant and distinct jewelry pieces, they bring a refined touch to any collection.

Cushion

The cushion cut diamond offers a captivating blend of vintage allure and contemporary elegance. With its rounded edges and brilliant facets, it has become a sought-after choice for those who appreciate timeless sophistication.

Marquise

The marquise cut diamond features a sleek, elongated shape with pointed ends, exuding a regal aura. Tejani Diam offers this distinctive design for those who seek elegance and uniqueness in their jewelry.

Emerald

Emerald cut diamonds at Tejani Diam highlight their clean lines and step cuts, creating a graceful and sophisticated appearance. Their unique clarity and elegance make them a perfect choice for refined tastes.

Princess

The princess cut diamond is a modern favorite, known for its sharp edges and unmatched brilliance. At Tejani Diam, our princess cut diamonds deliver exceptional sparkle and contemporary style.

Heart

The heart-shaped diamond is the ultimate symbol of love. Tejani Diam presents this romantic cut with precision and care, making it a heartfelt addition to any jewelry piece.
